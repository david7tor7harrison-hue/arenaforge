TOURNAMENT RULES

MATCH RULES & GUIDELINES

Read all instructions carefully before joining ArenaForge matches. Recording is compulsory from room joining until match completion.

Joining Instructions

  • Recording is compulsory from room joining until room starts. Failure to provide recording may result in direct ban with no excuses accepted.
  • Enter Account Name: Please enter your FF MAX account name correctly. No changes will be allowed later.
  • Verify Account Name: Your FF MAX account name must match your registered game name.
  • Match Room ID & Password: Room details will be shared 5–10 minutes before the match.
  • Refund Policy: No refunds for missed matches.

Eligibility Rules

  • Level Requirement: Level 40+ ID holders are eligible.
  • Headshot Rate: BR career headshot rate must not be suspicious.
  • Device Requirement: Android, iOS, tablets and phones only. (Emulators are not allowed.)

Prohibited Behavior

  • Aimbot
  • No Recoil
  • Hacking
  • Game Modifying Tools
  • Glitches
  • Bugs Exploitation
  • Teaming With Opponents
  • Inviting Unregistered Players
  • Double Vector Gun & M79 Gun

Gameplay Requirements

  • Mandatory Match Recording: Record matches properly. Failure to comply may result in penalties.
  • Prize Adjustment: Prize pools may be adjusted if tournament slots are not full.
  • Report Issues: Contact customer support within 1 hour after match completion.

Important Notes

  • Blacklisted Game-ID: Blacklisted Game IDs require recording proof for prizes.
  • Screen Recording: Record screens while joining custom rooms.
  • SkillClash Rights: ArenaForge reserves the right to modify prizes and rules anytime.
